Our services include project and operations management, compliance, cultural fitness assessment, title search and assessment, and a wide range of other consultancy services. We cater primarily to international business entities venturing into media investments that are getting ready to launch (or already launched) its business in the Middle East, North Africa and Turkey or require someone to advise on how to set or improve operational efficiency, project management protocols and compliance guidelines. While we cater to the whole media sector, we focus on catering to the gaming ,scripted and unscripted content production, broadcast, and music publishing companies.
