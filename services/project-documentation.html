Project Documentation

Our film production management consultancy streamlines the collection and preservation of your project's vital legal documents. We expertly manage contracts with financiers, distributors, and sales agents, alongside talent & crew agreements, insurance, and marketing materials. Our meticulous approach includes everything from release forms and short-form assignments to copyright records and trademark registrations. We emphasize the importance of a clear chain of title, safeguarding your project's ownership rights. Please note: this is a management consultancy service and not legal service.
