Cultural and Political Insights


We tailor consultancy and training programs relating to cultural and political fluency necessary to successfully operate and run any business or function in the Middle East, North Africa and Turkey.

Whether a private businesses, a non-profit organization or a governmental entity, we can ready your employees, contractors and agents to face the realities and shifting dynamics and landscapes of the MENAT region markets, through:


  • Providing in-depth analysis of political landscapes and propose risk mitigation strategies;
  • Providing guidance on navigating sensitive political environments and building trust with local stakeholders;
  • Setting up and conducting training on interpreting political discourse and cultural nuances;
  • Proposing strategies for engaging with political stakeholders and building productive relationships;
  • Adapting communication approaches for maximum impact and cultural relevance.


By investing in our training programs, you are equipping your team with the tools they need to understand and navigate the complexities of these dynamic markets. This knowledge translates to improved risk management, stronger relationships, and ultimately, greater success for your organization.
